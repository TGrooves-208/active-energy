What We Do

We provide design, installation, and maintenance of radiant heating systems, specializing in custom homes in the Bay Area and Northern Nevada. We are Eichler specialists, and we are very familiar with the specific problems related to those homes such as corrosion, leaking and poor heating performance. We install high efficiency boilers, service older units, and we can help with solar thermal systems related to radiant heat and domestic hot water. We also provide radiant heat inspections and reports during the sale of a home. These inspections include a system leak test, evaluation and consultation. We work closely with homeowners and builders to produce high quality, practical Radiant Heat systems. Active Energy

Formula 10

This product has been used for over twenty-five years in closed loop hydronic systems. It is an excellent corrosion inhibitor that is easy to use and has some added benefits: Formula 10 stops corrosion in iron pumps and other components caused by oxygen permeation through non-metallic radiant heat tubing, or supply piping. Modern PEX tubing, with an oxygen barrier, has been designed to stop molecular oxygen from passing through the tube wall and into systems, but it is not 100% effective, and over time can lead to rust buildup and clogging. Many systems were installed with tubing that had no oxygen barrier. Formula 10 is so effective at controlling corrosion that it allows iron pumps, boilers and piping to be used in hydronic systems of all designs. This product is also effective in drain back or drain down solar storage tanks where there can be iron, copper, brass and other metals exposed to oxygenated water.

Formula 10 does not deteriorate over time. Add it in the beginning, and enjoy its benefits for life! Do not mix Formula 10 with other chemicals or add it to systems already treated with other products. On new systems, Formula 10 can be added after the system is tested, drained, and ready to be refilled. Formula 10 System Preservative is stocked in quart, gallon, and five gallon containers, and is available for immediate shipment to any location.
